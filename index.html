Human with honey sauce

  • Time: Several hours
  • Serves: Me
Ingredients Quantity Talk about
Tender human One will be more than enough How's the weather...
Sweet honey Quite a few spoons Anything that is not family
Secret spices It's a secret Whisper nonsenses

Step 1: Dress up, your best day clothes. Go search a human.

Step 2: After finding your human, walk with your human in a park. Twice.

Step 3: At the park, be bit by mosquitoes.

Step 4: Still at the park, talk in french and in english about how's the weather and other stuff.

Step 5: Go to the sweetest places in your mind and hug your human goodnight.

Step 6: Sing. Sing a lot. Sing alone. Wish to sing with your human.

Step 7: Fill everything with sweet honey. With sweet words.

Step 8: Kiss your human lips.

Step 9: Enjoy anytime!

Don't even write about family in the recipe when copying this to your little notebook, BUT it's ok to write about "no" talking "about family" when teaching the recipe, like in a recipe website book.

Bonjour Cake

  • Time: A daily amount of time
  • Serves: Anyone
Ingredients Quantity Think about
Exercice More than 15 minutes What you can do to take care of yourself
Ambition More than you think, less than you fear Your goals
Good sleep Less than you want Dream
It may take several tries to make this one work, even I didn't perfect it yet.

Step 1 Prepare the exercice, work hard on it, make it work with your routine, more than 15 minutes everyday so you can get used to it.

Step 2 Pick a goal.

Step 3 Doesn't matter how long you will try it. Pick at least one goal and keep trying. If you give up, doesn't matter, pick another or even pick the same one again.

Step 4 Let a goal stick

Step 5 Achieve something from time to time.

Step 6 Sleep in the end, in the beginning, sleep in the middle. But sleep right. No more than you really need.

Step 7 Repeat every daily amount of hours.
